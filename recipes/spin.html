Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheeses
  • Garlic
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Artichoke hearts
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Transfer mixture to a prepared baking dish. Top with cheese
  3. Bake the spinach artichoke dip in preheated oven until bubbly
